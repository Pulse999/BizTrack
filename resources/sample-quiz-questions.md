## Tips for Creating More Quiz Questions

### Good Question Characteristics:
- ✅ Clear and specific
- ✅ Tests actual knowledge, not memory
- ✅ Has one obviously correct answer
- ✅ Includes plausible distractors
- ✅ Uses realistic scenarios

### Avoid:
- ❌ Trick questions
- ❌ Ambiguous wording
- ❌ "All of the above" / "None of the above"
- ❌ Questions that test reading comprehension rather than knowledge
- ❌ Questions with multiple correct answers

### Question Types to Include:
1. **Definition questions** - "What is...?"
2. **Procedure questions** - "How do you...?"
3. **Scenario questions** - "What should you do if...?"
4. **Application questions** - "When would you use...?"
5. **Best practice questions** - "What is the recommended way to...?"
